About Us

We are a black owned business run by college students with a dream of putting a smile on everyone's faces one cup of drink(non-alcoholic) and one food item at a time. We use naturally sourced ingredients and pay attention to dietary choices. Our owners are Adebola, Farouk, Jonathan.

Our coffee beans are imported directly from Columbia, where almost 15% of the world’s coffee beans come from. All ingredients of animal origin used on our menu are directly purchased from free range farms.
